# Privacy Policy — HopeTSIT

**Last updated: April 18, 2026**
**Version: 1.0**

HopeTSIT Limited, a company registered in Hong Kong ("HopeTSIT", "we"), cares deeply about protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how you can exercise your rights.

This policy complies with the **Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO)** of Hong Kong and with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** for Users located in the European Economic Area.

## 1. Data controller

The data controller is:

- **HopeTSIT Limited**
- Jurisdiction: Hong Kong (PDPO)
- Data Protection Officer email: privacy@hopetsit.com

## 2. Data we collect

### 2.1 Data you provide directly

- First name, last name, email, phone number
- Date of birth (age verification)
- Postal address or city of residence
- Profile picture
- Pet information (name, species, breed, age, photo)
- ID documents for verification (ID card, passport) — only for sitters and walkers
- IBAN or payment details (via Stripe)
- Messages exchanged through the in-app chat
- Reports posted on the PawMap (text, optional photos)

### 2.2 Data collected automatically

- IP address, device type, operating system, app version
- Geolocation data **when you enable** map, pet-tracking or live-walk features
- Usage logs (actions performed, screens visited)
- Advertising identifiers (only with your consent)

### 2.3 Payment data

Bank information (card number, CVV) is **never stored** on our servers. It is processed exclusively by **Stripe, Inc.** (PCI-DSS Level 1). We only keep the transaction ID and metadata needed for accounting.

## 3. Purposes

We use your data to:

- Create and manage your account
- Match owners, sitters and walkers
- Process payments and payouts
- Display relevant points of interest around you on the PawMap
- Send notifications (bookings, messages, reports)
- Verify providers' identity (legal obligation and user safety)
- Prevent fraud and moderate content
- Improve the Service (anonymised statistics)
- Respond to your support requests
- Comply with our legal and accounting obligations

## 4. Legal bases (GDPR)

Under GDPR article 6, our processing relies on:

- **Performance of the contract** between you and HopeTSIT
- **Your consent** for precise geolocation, push notifications and analytics cookies
- **Our legitimate interest** in preventing fraud and improving the Service
- **Compliance with legal obligations** (accounting, anti-money-laundering, responding to competent authorities)

## 5. Recipients

Your data may be shared with:

- Other Users, strictly to the extent necessary for the match (e.g. your first name and city are visible when a booking is made)
- Our contracted sub-processors (Stripe for payments, hosting providers, email provider, notification provider)
- Judicial or administrative authorities upon lawful request
- A potential acquirer in the event of a sale of the business

We **never sell** your data to third parties for advertising purposes.

## 6. International transfers

Since HopeTSIT is based in Hong Kong, some data may be transferred outside the European Economic Area. In such cases, we put in place appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses, adequacy decisions where available) to ensure an equivalent level of protection.

## 7. Retention

- **Active account**: for the full duration of Service use
- **Closed account**: deletion within 30 days, subject to legal retention (accounting: 7 years in Hong Kong; invoicing: 10 years)
- **PawMap reports**: 48 hours of active visibility; indefinite retention in anonymised form for analytics
- **Technical logs**: 12 months maximum
- **Messages**: retained for the lifetime of the account, then deleted

## 8. Your rights

At any time, you have the right to:

- **Access** your data
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** processing
- **Portability**: receive your data in a structured format
- **Object** to certain processing (e.g. profiling)
- **Withdraw consent** at any time
- **Lodge a complaint** with a supervisory authority (CNIL in France, ICO in the United Kingdom, Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data in Hong Kong)

To exercise these rights, contact us at **privacy@hopetsit.com**. We will reply within 30 days maximum.

## 9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data: TLS in transit, encryption at rest for sensitive fields, strict access control, audit logging, regular audits. Payments go exclusively through Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1).

In the event of a data breach likely to pose a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will inform you as soon as possible, in compliance with GDPR article 34.

## 10. Cookies and trackers

The mobile app uses a persistent technical identifier needed for the Service to function. Analytics and advertising cookies are only activated with your explicit consent at first launch.

You can change your choices at any time from the app settings.

## 11. Minors

The Service is restricted to adults (18 and over). We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you notice a minor using the Service, please report it to **privacy@hopetsit.com** so we can delete the account.

## 12. Changes

This policy may be updated to reflect legal or functional changes. Material changes will be notified in-app and by email.

## 13. Contact

For any data-related question or request:

- General email: privacy@hopetsit.com
- User support: support@hopetsit.com
- Hong Kong Privacy Commissioner: https://www.pcpd.org.hk/
